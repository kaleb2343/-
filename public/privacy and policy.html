About ምእራፍ

Welcome to ምእራፍ! We created this platform to help writers and creators share their work easily and freely. Whether you're an author looking to publish or a reader looking for new stories, our goal is to connect creativity with community.

Users can:

  • Sign up quickly
  • Upload books with details like title, author, genre, cover, and description
  • Share PDF books for free
  • Have fun exploring and creating!

We use the information we collect to:
